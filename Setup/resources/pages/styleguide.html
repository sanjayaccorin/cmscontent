AERIN is a global luxury lifestyle brand inspired by the signature style of its founder, Accorin Lauder. AERIN is a global luxury lifestyle brand inspired by the signature style of its founder, Accorin Lauder. With a passion for art, travel, fashion, and design, Accorin’s own lifestyle serves as a focal point of inspiration for the brand. Classic, but always with a modern point of view, every piece is created to make life more beautiful, with a sense of ease and refinement.
